I interviewed my friend Ami Lucille who wanted a  webpage for artists.
Her target audience are artists and people interested in art and creativity .
The website should act like an artistic world where creators can share artwork, interact with each other, buy and sell art pieces and discover inspiration.
Lucille preferred a strong French aesthetic inspired by vintage posters , elegant typography of france and the artistic culture in General.
She wanted the design to feel  aesthetic welcoming and creative and with vibrant colors. i proposed  some ideas to her like a black border(bold) which she quickly agreed to.
I added a call to action form where other artist create there profile and join the vast world of art.
